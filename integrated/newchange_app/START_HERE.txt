📱 PHONE ACCESS (FOR TEACHER TESTING)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

To let your teacher test on her phone:

1. Make sure both of you are on the SAME WiFi network
2. Find your computer's IP address:
   - Double-click: find_my_ip.bat
   - OR type in cmd: ipconfig
   - Look for IPv4 Address (like 192.168.1.100)

3. Share this URL with teacher:
   http://YOUR-IP-ADDRESS:5000
   (Replace YOUR-IP-ADDRESS with the actual IP you found)

Example: If your IP is 192.168.1.100
Share: http://192.168.1.100:5000

⚠️  IMPORTANT: 
- Your computer must stay ON and running the app
- Both devices must be on SAME WiFi
- XAMPP MySQL must be running
